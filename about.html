About Us

Canoeing Club is a beginner-friendly community focused on safety, skill-building, and enjoying nature together.

Our Mission

We help people of all levels discover canoeing in a safe, supportive, and welcoming environment. Whether you’re trying it for the first time or improving technique, you’ll find friendly guidance and great routes.

Safety first Certified guidance and clear rules.
Beginner focused Step-by-step learning, no pressure.
Community Meet people who love the outdoors.

What We Do

We organize regular training sessions, calm lake paddles, and occasional longer touring trips. Members can borrow equipment and join events that match their experience level.

Courses Basics, safety, and technique practice.
Events Sunset paddles, meetups, and club days.
Trips Scenic routes with careful planning.

Our Values

Respect nature, support each other, and always paddle responsibly. We aim to create a calm, inclusive environment where everyone feels safe on the water.
